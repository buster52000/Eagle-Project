In 1928, the Mixsell sisters gifted the Northampton County Historical and Genealogical Society with their grandfather's house.  For 80 years,t he house was the home of the Society until its move to the Sigal Museum in 2010.   The Mixsell house is in the process of becoming a Textile Research Facility and Museum.
