Maker of music

Christian F. Martin Sr was born in Germany and apprenticed at the age of 15 to a renown guitar maker in Vienna, Austria.  He was a gifted apprentice and constructed guitars of exceptional quality.  Martin Guitars was started by C. F. Martin Sr in 1833 in New York City.  In 1938 Martin moved his business to Nazareth, PA.   For over 150 years, six generations of the Martin family have continued to produce the finest instruments available.
