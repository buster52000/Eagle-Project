Just Born
Sam Born opens a small factory in Brooklyn,NY, that markets its freshness with a sign that declares the cany is "just born."  In 1932, Just Born Co moves to Bethlehem, PA.  After 75 years, this family-owned candy manufacturer makes PEEPS, MIKE AND IKE, HOT TAMALES, JIMMIES, PEANUT CHEWS, & TEENEE BEANEE. Sold internationally, all candies are made in the USA.
